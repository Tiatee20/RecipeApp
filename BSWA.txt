Baked Salmon with Asparagus
dinner
1hr
intermediate-meals,6-ingredients
ingredients
4 salmon fillets
1 lb asparagus
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
instructions
Preheat the oven to 400Â°F.
Trim the ends of the asparagus and place them in a single layer on a baking sheet.
Drizzle the asparagus with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, lemon juice, and garlic powder. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Toss the asparagus to coat, then push them to the sides of the baking sheet.
Place the salmon fillets in the center of the baking sheet and season them with salt and pepper.
Drizzle the remaining tablespoon of olive oil over the salmon fillets.
Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the asparagus is tender.
Serve the salmon with the roasted asparagus.
