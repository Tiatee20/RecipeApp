Spaghetti and Meatballs
lunch
1hr
medium-meals,16-ingredients
ingredients
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 egg, beaten
2 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and pepper, to taste
Sauce
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
Salt and pepper, to taste
1 pound spaghetti
Salt, for pasta water
instructions
Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, egg, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
Form mixture into 16-20 meatballs and place on prepared baking sheet. Bake for 20 minutes, or until browned and cooked through.
Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 10-15 minutes.
Cook spaghetti according to package directions in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
Serve spaghetti topped with meatballs and sauce.
